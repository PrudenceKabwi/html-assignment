Lisa Manoban

Lisa Manoban,known as Lisa from BLACKPINK is a talented rapper and dancer. She begun her career in August 2016 and later realesed her solo single albumLalisa through YG entertainment.Her full-length debut solo album,Alter Ego ,was released on February 2025. She did collabs with great artists like DojaCat,Raye,Rosalia,Future,Megan Thee Stallion and even Tyla. Outside of being a great artist Lisa is also an ambassador of fashion brands like Louis Vuitton,Bvlgari and MAC Cosmetics. She encourages people to dream big since she was also just a young girl from Thailand who loved music.

Some of her notable quotes are:"Never give up on your dreams,no matter how painful and difficult your journey is,if you trust in yourself and fight for it until the end,your dreams will come true."
